About Us

About Us

Welcome to the National MCA Caucus website, the premier platform for Members of County Assemblies (MCAs) across Kenya. Our mission is to unite and empower MCAs to effectively serve their constituents and enhance governance at the county level.

"Alone, we can do so little; together, we can do so much‚Äù

The National MCA Caucus serves as a critical forum for collaboration among MCAs from various counties, fostering dialogue and sharing best practices. We aim to strengthen the legislative framework and advocate for the interests of our members, ensuring that the voices of the people are heard and represented.

Advocacy

We advocate for the rights and welfare of MCAs, pushing for better terms of service, including salary reviews, office allowances, and financial autonomy.

Capacity Building

We provide training and resources to enhance the skills and effectiveness of MCAs in their legislative roles.

Networking

Our caucus facilitates networking opportunities among MCAs, promoting collaboration and the sharing of experiences.

Policy Development

We engage in policy discussions that impact our counties, ensuring that our members contribute to shaping legislation that benefits the public.

Our Representatives
